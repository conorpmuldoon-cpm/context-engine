SYRACUSE, N.Y. — Syracuse's Common Council approved the sale of a former station on East Fayette Street in Syracuse, which will be transformed into housing.

The former station will undergo a massive $900,000 transformation after being out of commission for over a decade.

Syracuse-based company JF Real Estate plans to transform the old building into four apartments and a restaurant, with multi-million-dollar renovations.
