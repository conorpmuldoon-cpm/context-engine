SYRACUSE, N.Y. — Several inches of snow are expected across much of Central New York on Friday and Friday night.

The City of Syracuse is one of many locations that can see significant snowfall.

If you plan to travel to Syracuse, you may need to know whether the city's streets are plowed. The city's Snow Operations Map details how recently a plow cleared the road. It also shows the locations of illegally parked vehicles.
