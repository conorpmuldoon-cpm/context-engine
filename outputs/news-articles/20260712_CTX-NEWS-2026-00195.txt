The Onondaga County Clerk’s office registered 91 home sales during the week of June 28 to July 4. The most expensive home on the list was sold by former Syracuse University basketball coach Adrian Autry and his wife for $1,350,000. The home is a four bedroom, five bath spread over more than 4,500 square feet. It sits on a 1.14 acre lot in Jamesville. See photo of the home.
