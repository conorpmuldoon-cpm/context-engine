Village of North Syracuse gives little notice for taking away trash cans

NORTH SYRACUSE, N.Y. — The Village of North Syracuse residents have experienced headaches regarding their trash pickups.

The village is switching to a new trash removal company after the contract with Syracuse Haulers has recently ended.

Syracuse Haulers came by to residences this week to take away people's trash bins.

The new company, Butler Disposal, will begin distributing new trash cans starting June 5th. Until then, residents will not have a trash bin to take their trash out with.

“Anytime i've had exposed trash for any longer than a couple hours that means that raccoons or squirrels can get into it and that creates a mess and just doesn’t look good," North Syracuse neighbor Jill Lewandowska said.

Neighbors expressed frustration with the village's lack of communication. Some even said they had no idea their trash cans were taken.

“It's gonna be a mess and an eye sore for a week or two," North Syracuse neighbor, Joe Asztalos said.

Residents were notified after their bin was taken by a note left on their front door.
