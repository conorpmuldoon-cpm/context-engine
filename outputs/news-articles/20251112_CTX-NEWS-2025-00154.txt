SYRACUSE, N.Y. — The city of Syracuse is hosting a pair of open houses on the I-81 Viaduct project.

The events are part of the city’s Reconnecting Communities Planning Project, which focuses on five southside corridors: Colvin Street, Taylor Street, MLK Street, Adams and Harrison streets, and the proposed Linear Park planned between Roesler and Wilson parks.

The first open house is on Wednesday, November 12 at Beauchamp Branch Library’s upstairs event space located at 2111 S. Salina Street.

The second open house is on Thursday, November 13 at the Pioneer Homes Coffee Shop, located at 1001 S. McBride Street.

Both open house run from 4:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.
