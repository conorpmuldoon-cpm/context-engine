Syracuse, N.Y. — Two people were taken into custody Sunday after shots were fired multiple times on the South Side of Syracuse, police said. Around 8:16 p.m., officers responded to multiple ShotSpotter alerts reporting possible gunfire in the area, according to Sgt. Thomas Blake, a spokesperson for the Syracuse Police Department. The ShotSpotter caught several shots fired in the 100 block of Grape Terrace and the 100 block of Danforth Terrace, he said.
