SYRACUSE, N.Y. — A major announcement from Brooklyn Pickle on the morning of its 50th anniversary of operating the sandwich shop in Syracuse.

A new Brooklyn Pickle location is coming to Marshall Street on the Syracuse University campus, according to Craig Kowadla, the owner of Brooklyn Pickle.

This is the latest expansion for the business, after expanding to a Utica location and North Carolina location in recent years.

There are a few locations in the Syracuse area as well.

To celebrate the 50th anniversary, Brooklyn Pickle is offering a number of discounts on food for Thursday, July 17.
