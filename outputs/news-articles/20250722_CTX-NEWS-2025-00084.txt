SKANEATELES, N.Y. — Rachel DeWitt arrived to Clift Park in Skaneateles just before sunrise on Tuesday with a large group of supporters with her.

DeWitt, a Skaneateles native, plans to swim the length of Skaneateles Lake, with the goal to raise awareness and money to support the Skaneateles Lake Association.

The lake is about 16 miles long. DeWitt said it would take her about 11 hours to go end-to-end.

DeWitt told CNY Central that she had raised about $7,000 heading into Tuesday, to support the association's efforts to keep the lake clean. Skaneateles Lake is a major drinking water source for the City of Syracuse and other surrounding communities.
