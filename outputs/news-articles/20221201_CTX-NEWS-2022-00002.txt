DOWNTOWN SYRACUSE
Downtown Syracuse is easy-to-navigate!

Whatever it is that brings you into Downtown, you’ll find Downtown Syracuse is walkable or easy-to-navigate by bicycle, car or bus!

Bicyclists will also find our city is easy to navigate on two wheels. The Empire State Trail now runs through Downtown Syracuse! 

Drivers can choose from an array of affordable, easy-to-find parking options – available on-street, and in surface lots and garages. Download our Downtown Syracuse Parking Guide to learn more.

Prefer public transportation? Check out our excellent and accommodating Centro Bus Service!
