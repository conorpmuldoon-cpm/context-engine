West Haven, Conn. — Graduate student attacker Francesca Tortora (Syracuse, N.Y./Cicero-North Syracuse/Stony Brook) scored the game-winning goal with 1:28 remaining in double overtime to give the Le Moyne College women’s lacrosse team a 12-11 victory over the University of New Haven on Saturday afternoon in NEC action at Kathy Zolad Stadium. The Dolphins played a double overtime contest for the first time since a 12-11 loss to Adelphi University in the NCAA Championship Quarterfinals on May 12, 2019.
