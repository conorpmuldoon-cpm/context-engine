Syracuse, N.Y. — A Syracuse common councilor is joining with the city auditor in asking lawmakers to approve a new parking violation aimed at bicycle safety.
Common Councilor Corey Williams has introduced legislation establishing “Blocking a Bicycle Lane” as a standalone traffic code violation in the city.
