DeWitt, N.Y. - Le Moyne College has extended its president’s contract for two more years, the college announced.
Le Moyne’s Board of Trustees recently approved a two-year extension on President Linda LeMura’s contract, according to a news release. That means her contract will run through June 30, 2028, rather ending at the end of June.
In December, LeMura will become the longest serving president in Le Moyne’s history. LeMura was appointed in 2014 as the college’s 14th president.
LeMura came to Le Moyne
as the dean of the College of Arts and Sciences in 2003. She held that position for four years before being named provost and vice president for academic affairs in 2007. She was provost until becoming president in 2014.
