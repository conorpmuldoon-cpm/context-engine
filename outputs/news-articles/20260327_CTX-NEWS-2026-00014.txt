Syracuse, N.Y. — A Syracuse University-based research group won a major court ruling Tuesday requiring federal immigration agencies to release detailed enforcement data. The Transactional Records Access Clearinghouse (TRAC) and its co-director, Susan B. Long, won a federal FOIA lawsuit against U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement and U.S. Customs and Border Protection. Long is also an associate professor of managerial statistics at Syracuse University.
