Junior midfielder Maddi Barney scored a career-high five goals to lead Le Moyne College to a 22-8 victory over Central Connecticut State in Saturday’s women’s lacrosse regular season finale at Arute Field. The victory gave the Dolphins a share of the Northeast Conference regular season championship and secured the top seed for the upcoming NEC Championship tournament. Le Moyne finished 13-3 overall and 8-1 in conference play.
