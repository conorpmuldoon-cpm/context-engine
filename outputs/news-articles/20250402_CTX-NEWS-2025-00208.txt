By
Tim Knauss | tknauss@syracuse.com
Syracuse, N.Y. – Syracuse Mayor Ben Walsh’s administration today filed a lawsuit against a Chicago investment group who were
welcomed to town
two years ago when they promised to revive the notorious Skyline Apartments.
Clear Investment Group, which bought the Skyline in 2023 along with three other sorely neglected properties formerly owned by
Tim and Troy Green
, has itself neglected the properties, city officials said in lawsuit filed in state Supreme Court.
