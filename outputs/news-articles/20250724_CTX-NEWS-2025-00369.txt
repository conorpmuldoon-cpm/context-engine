SYRACUSE, N.Y. — A new splashpad opened at Cummings Field.

The new splashpad was built in place of old baseball fields that were decommissioned due to drainage issues.

On Thursday, Syracuse Commissioner of Parks, Recreation & Youth Programs Syeisha Bird said the Eastwood Association recommended the splashpad in 2021. She pointed out that the new splashpad is based on baseball.
