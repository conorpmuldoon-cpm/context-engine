SYRACUSE, N.Y. — Friday, December 5, 2025 will be known as 'Freedom of Espresso Day' in the City of Syracuse.

The popular coffee business, which now has locations in Syracuse, Liverpool, Fayetteville and Camillus, is being recognized for opening its first Syracuse location 30 years ago.

Syracuse Mayor Ben Walsh will visit the Solar Street location to make the proclamation at 8 a.m. on Friday.

The business said on Facebook for customers to come "celebrate with [them], enjoy a cup of coffee, and be part of this very special moment for our community.
