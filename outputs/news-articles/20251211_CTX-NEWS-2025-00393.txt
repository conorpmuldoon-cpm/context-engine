SYRACUSE, N.Y. (WSYR-TV) — Syracuse Mayor Ben Walsh delivered his last community briefing at City Hall before his term is up on Dec. 31.
One of the goals of Walsh’s administration over the years has been prioritizing public safety and getting crime numbers down. The Syracuse Police Department confirmed to city officials that overall crime is down by over 20%.
Walsh said there have been “significant decreases in both violent crime and property crime, year over year.”
