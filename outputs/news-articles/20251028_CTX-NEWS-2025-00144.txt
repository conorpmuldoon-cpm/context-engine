SYRACUSE, N.Y. — The Syracuse Common Council approved plans for the former Syracuse Developmental Center in a unanimous vote.

All of the points proposed for the housing project that will take its place were approved by the council.

This includes plans for funding, demolition of the existing property and phases one and two of the construction process.

The Albanese Organization has been working with the city on this project and is expected to issue construction bids for infrastructure, new utilities, roadways and more in November.
