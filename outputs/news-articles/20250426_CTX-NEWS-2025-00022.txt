Syracuse, N.Y. — A Friday night public hearing scheduled for 6 p.m. at the Syracuse Housing Authority main office lasted all of 20 minutes.

The hearing was open for comments on the draft annual plan, a document public housing authorities like SHA submit.

There were two CNY Central journalists, one other member of the press and a community advocate in attendance, plus two public housing residents.
