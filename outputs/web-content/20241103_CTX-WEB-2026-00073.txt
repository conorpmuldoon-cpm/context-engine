Mayor Walsh Announces Road Reconstruction Project on Milton Avenue

Published on November 03, 2024

Syracuse Mayor Ben Walsh announced a road reconstruction project on Milton Avenue beginning November 4, 2024. Crews will mill and pave Milton Avenue from West Genesee Street to the city line. The road will remain open during construction which will take place from 6 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Drivers may experience delays and are advised to use alternate routes if possible. The project is expected to be completed in about one week; actual project timeframe is contingent upon favorable weather.
