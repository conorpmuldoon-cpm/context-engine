Traffic Advisory & Street Closure Notice for Memorial Day Parade

Published on May 19, 2026

The Eastwood Neighborhood Association will host the Eastwood Memorial Day Parade on Monday, May 25, 2026.  
 
The parade will begin at 9:00 a.m. at James Street and Nichols Avenue and proceed west down James Street, ending at the intersection of James Street and Grant Boulevard. The parade will conclude at approximately 9:30 a.m. with a memorial ceremony to follow at the Greenway Veterans’ Memorial Park, 2309 James Street.  
 
To facilitate the setup, presentation and breakdown of this event the following street closure will take place at approximately 8:30 a.m., with no parking permitted:  

James Street between Nichols Ave and Grant Boulevard 

 

For Media Inquiries

For media inquiries, members of the press should contact the Office of Communications by emailing press@syr.gov.
