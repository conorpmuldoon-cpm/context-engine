City Department of Water Responding to Low Pressure in Water System

Published on March 06, 2025

The City of Syracuse Department of Water is responding to low pressure in the water system impacting high-elevation areas and high-rise buildings. The Department is investigating sources of water loss in the system and is asking residents to report any signs of potential water leaks, such as water emerging from beneath roads or on private property. As the investigation continues, the Department is increasing supply to the City which is expected to increase water pressure in impacted areas. Reports can be made by calling the Water Department at 315-448-8360.
