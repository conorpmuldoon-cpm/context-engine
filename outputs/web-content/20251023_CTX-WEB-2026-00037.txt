City of Syracuse Announces Delay in Trash Collection on Southside.

Published on October 23, 2025

The City of Syracuse Department of Public Works (DPW) informs residents that trash will be delayed on Thursday in some parts of the Southside on the following streets: 
Crescent Ave 
Morton Ave 
Eloise Ter  
 Onondaga Ave 
Parkside Ave 
Coolidge Ave  
Palmer Ave 
 Bellevue Ave  
Cheney St 
Sterling Ave 
Residents are encouraged to keep their trash carts out as crews work to empty remaining carts. Crews will pick up at remaining households Friday, October 24. 
Residents are encouraged to monitor the social media channels @SYRCityline for further updates on rescheduled pickups. 

 

 

For Media Inquiries

For media inquiries, members of the press should contact the Office of Communications by emailing press@syr.gov.
