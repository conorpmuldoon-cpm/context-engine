Mayor Owens Announces Road Reconstruction Project on S Salina St

Published on June 08, 2026

Syracuse Mayor Sharon F. Owens announced a road reconstruction project beginning on Monday, June 8 on South Salina Street. The project will include milling and paving work on South Salina Street between Ballantyne Road and West Seneca Turnpike. During construction, traffic will be reduced to one lane, and work will take place in phases between 6 a.m. and 6 p.m. Drivers may experience delays and are advised to use alternate routes if possible. The project is expected to be completed in about one week; actual project timeframe is contingent upon favorable weather.
