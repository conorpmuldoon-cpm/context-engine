Water Crews Working to Repair Water Main Break on E Washington St

Published on December 28, 2024

The City of Syracuse Department of Water is working to repair a water main on the 100 block of East Washington Street. Households and businesses in the area will experience water service disruptions till late tomorrow morning. East Washington Street between South Warren Street and South Salina Street, as well as South Warren Street between East Fayette Street and East Washington Street will be closed to all vehicle traffic until repair work is completed. Sidewalks remain open and traffic detours are in place. Residents and businesses with questions or concerns can contact the Department of Water at 315-448-8360.

For Media Inquiries

For media inquiries, members of the press should contact the Office of Communications by emailing press@syr.gov.
