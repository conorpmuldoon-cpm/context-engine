2023 Irish Festival Street Closing Information

Published on September 05, 2023

Syracuse, N.Y. - Clinton Square will be hosting the Irish Festival on Friday, September 8 and Saturday, September 9.

The following streets will be closed for the festival from 9:30 a.m. on Thursday, September 7 through 12:00 p.m. Sunday, September 10:

Clinton Street, from Genesee Street to Washington Street
Erie Boulevard West, from Clinton Street to Franklin Street
West Water Street, from Clinton Street to Franklin Street, will become a two-way with no parking along the north curb line.

For more information on the Syracuse Irish Festival please visit www.syracuseirishfestival.com
