Sewer Department to Complete Repair Work on S Clinton St on Feb 18

Published on February 16, 2024

City of Syracuse Sewer Department will complete repair work on South Clinton Street between West Fayette Street and Walton Street on Sunday, Feb. 18 starting at 7 am. The road will be closed to all vehicle traffic until repair work is completed. Sidewalks will remain open and traffic detours will be in place. Residents with questions or concerns can contact Cityline at (315) 448-CITY (2489) or use the SYRCityline app.

 

For Media Inquiries

For media inquiries, members of the press should contact the Office of Communications by emailing press@syr.gov.
