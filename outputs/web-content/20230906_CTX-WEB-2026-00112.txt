Mayor Walsh Announces Road Reconstruction on Martin Luther King East

Published on September 06, 2023

SYRACUSE, N.Y. – Syracuse Mayor Ben Walsh announced a road reconstruction project on Martin Luther King East to begin on Thursday, September 7. Crews will mill and pave Martin Luther King East between South Salina Street and Renwick Avenue.  

The road will be closed to through traffic except for intersections at South State Street and Oakwood Avenue. The actual project timeframe is contingent on favorable weather.
