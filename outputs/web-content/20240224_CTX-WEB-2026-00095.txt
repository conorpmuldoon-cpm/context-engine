Water Crews Working to Repair Water Main Break on North Clinton Street

Published on February 24, 2024

The City of Syracuse Department of Water is working to repair a water main at the intersection of North Clinton Street and West Division Street. Water service in the area of the 600 block of North Clinton Street is disrupted. Water service is expected to return by 8 pm. The intersection of North Clinton Street and West Division Street will be closed off to all vehicle traffic until repair work is completed. Sidewalks remain open and traffic detours are in place. Residents with questions or concerns can contact the Department of Water at (315) 448-8360.
