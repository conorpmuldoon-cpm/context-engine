Street Closing Information for 2023 Mary Nelson's Youth Day

Published on August 15, 2023

Syracuse, N.Y. - The following streets will be closed for Mary Nelson’s 2023 Youth Day and Annual School Supply Giveaway in and around Sankofa Park on Saturday, August 19, 2023:

S. Salina Street, from E. Beard Ave to E. Colvin Street, will be closed from 8 a.m. to 7 p.m.
E. Colvin Street, from S. State Street to S. Salina Street, will be closed from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Wood Ave, from Mark Ave to S. Salina Street, will be closed from 8 a.m. to 7 p.m.
