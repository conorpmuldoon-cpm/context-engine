Water Department Closes S Warren St for Fire Service Repair

Published on August 08, 2024

The City of Syracuse Department of Water will be closing Warren Street between Jefferson Street and Fayette Street to facilitate repairs to a fire service. Residents and businesses in the area will not experience water service disruptions. Warren Street will be closed off to all vehicle traffic until repair work is completed. Vehicle exits from parking garages will be allowed. Sidewalks remain open and traffic detours are in place. Residents and businesses with questions or concerns can contact the Department of Water at (315) 448-8360.

 

For Media Inquiries

For media inquiries, members of the press should contact the Office of Communications by emailing press@syr.gov.
