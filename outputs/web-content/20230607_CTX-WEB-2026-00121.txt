City Announces Lane Restrictions for Greenspace Maintenance on West St

Published on June 07, 2023

Syracuse, N.Y. – City of Syracuse announced a greenspace maintenance project for West Street South between the 200 and 300 block of West Fayette Street. Crews will be removing existing shrubs and prepping the area on Saturday, June 10. Wildflower seeds will be planted at a later date.  

The two right lanes of West Street South will be closed from 6 a.m. to 1 p.m. The far-left lane will remain open while the work is being completed.
