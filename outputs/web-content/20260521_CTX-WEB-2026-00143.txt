City of Syracuse Closing Offices in Observance of Memorial Day Holiday

Published on May 21, 2026

City of Syracuse administrative offices will be closed for the Memorial Day holiday on Monday, May 25. Trash and recycling pickup will be delayed by one day for the entire week.

The Department of Public Works yard waste self-drop off, located at 1200 Canal St., will be closed to the public on Monday, May 25. Emergency public works services can be reported to 315-448-8360. Cityline offices will be closed for calls, but residents can still submit service requests to Cityline via the SYRCityline app or online at syr.gov/cityline.    

Metered parking in the city will be free on Monday, May 25.

 

For Media Inquiries

For media inquiries, members of the press should contact the Office of Communications by emailing press@syr.gov.
