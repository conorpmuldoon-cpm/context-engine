Water Crews Working to Repair Water Main Leak on Midland Avenue

Published on May 09, 2024

The City of Syracuse Department of Water is working to repair a water main on the intersection of West Colvin Street and Midland Avenue. Water service disruptions are expected in the area while repairs are taking place. Traffic on West Colvin Street is already impacted by an ongoing Onondaga County infrastructure project. Residents and businesses with questions or concerns can contact the Department of Water at (315) 448-8360.
